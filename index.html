To Put It Simply

Unless you're an eight year old girl writing a poem about unicorns, you should never use comic sans!

Fonts such as this makes it hard for the reader to take your content seriously.

Never fear!

Resources are available—Google Fonts and Fontsquirrel are just a couple options that provide some ttf and otf fonts free of charge. Beware of large "DOWNLOAD" buttons! These can be sneaky little unwanted friends that will wreak havoc on your system. #personalexperience
